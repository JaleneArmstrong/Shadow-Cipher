How To Play

| 1 |

Welcome to ShadowCipher!

You goal is to decode the a secret number chosen by your opponent within a limited number of guesses.

Can you crack the code?

| 2 |

To make a guess, enter a digit from 0-9 into each available slot, then press 'SUBMIT'.

Note: Repeated use of the same digit and non-numerical characters are not allowed.

| 3 |

After each guess, clues are given in the form of three (3) distinct values:

'Shuriken' -> The digit is fully correct.
'Kunai' -> The digit is correct, but in the wrong position.
'Smoke' -> The digit is fully incorrect.

| 4 |

Additionally, each digit in the clue is colour-coded accordingly:

Green -> 'Shuriken'
Yellow -> 'Kunai'
Brown -> 'Smoke'

| 5 |

With each guess, use the feedback to refine your strategy and get closer to victory.

| 6 |

Keep guessing and analyzing until you've cracked the code!
